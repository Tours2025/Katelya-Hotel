Step back in time as you explore Istanbul's historic peninsula, the heart of ancient Constantinople. Our Old City Tour I takes you through the most significant landmarks of Istanbul's UNESCO World Heritage area, where empires rose and fell over millennia.

Led by our expert guides, you'll discover the architectural marvels of Hagia Sophia, the Blue Mosque, and Topkapi Palace - each telling a unique story of Istanbul's rich history. Walk through the impressive grounds of these remarkable structures as your guide shares fascinating historical insights and cultural context.

This half-day tour is perfect for first-time visitors to Istanbul who want to experience the city's most iconic historical sites with knowledgeable guidance. Our small group format ensures a personalized experience and plenty of opportunities to ask questions.

Tour Itinerary

09:00 AM

Hotel Pickup

Pickup from your hotel in the city center or meeting at a central location.

09:30 AM

Hagia Sophia

Visit the magnificent Hagia Sophia, once a church, then a mosque, now a museum. Marvel at its enormous dome and stunning Byzantine mosaics.

10:45 AM

Blue Mosque (Sultan Ahmed Mosque)

Explore this stunning 17th-century mosque known for its beautiful blue Iznik tiles and six minarets.

11:45 AM

Hippodrome of Constantinople

Walk through the ancient Hippodrome and see the Obelisk of Theodosius, Serpent Column, and German Fountain.

12:15 PM

Topkapi Palace

Visit the primary residence of Ottoman sultans for 400 years, including the Treasury, Imperial Harem, and beautiful courtyards.

01:30 PM

Return Transfer

Return to your hotel or enjoy recommendations for local dining options in the area.
