Located east of Istanbul, Sapanca Lake is a freshwater lake surrounded by lush forests and mountains. The area is famous for its natural beauty, clean air, and tranquil atmosphere, making it a perfect escape from the busy city life.

Our Sapanca Lake Tour combines relaxation and adventure, giving you the opportunity to enjoy the serene waters of the lake, visit charming local villages, explore the natural surroundings, and taste delicious local food. Throughout the day, you'll get to witness the perfect harmony between nature and local culture in this picturesque region.

This tour is perfect for nature lovers, photographers, and anyone looking for a peaceful day in beautiful surroundings just a short drive from Istanbul.
