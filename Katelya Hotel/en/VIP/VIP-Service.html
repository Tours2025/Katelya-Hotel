Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the VIP transfer service?

Our VIP transfer service includes a luxury vehicle with a professional driver, meet and greet at the airport, flight monitoring, waiting time in case of delays, and door-to-door service to your destination.

How do I book a VIP transfer?

You can book your VIP transfer through our hotel reception, by clicking on one of the "Book Now" buttons on this page, or by contacting our concierge service directly.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

Don't worry about delays! We monitor all flight arrivals. Your driver will be informed about any delays and will adjust the pickup time accordingly, waiting for you at no extra charge.
