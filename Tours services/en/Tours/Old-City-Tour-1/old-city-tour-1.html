Old City Tour 1: Asia & Europe Full Day

Experience the best of Istanbul in one day with our comprehensive Old City Tour that spans both the European and Asian sides of this magnificent city. This tour offers a perfect introduction to Istanbul's rich history, stunning architecture, and vibrant culture.

Tour Highlights:

  • Hagia Sophia - Visit this architectural marvel that served as a church, mosque, and now a museum
  • Blue Mosque (Sultan Ahmed Mosque) - Explore one of the most iconic mosques known for its blue tiles
  • Hippodrome - Walk through this ancient chariot racing stadium and see the Obelisk of Theodosius
  • Grand Bazaar - Shop at one of the world's largest and oldest covered markets with over 4,000 shops
  • Topkapi Palace - Discover the opulent residence of Ottoman Sultans for nearly 400 years
  • Bosphorus Cruise - Enjoy a scenic ferry ride between Europe and Asia
  • Üsküdar - Visit this historic district on the Asian side with beautiful mosques and local markets
  • Maiden's Tower - See this iconic tower located on a small islet at the entrance to the Bosphorus
  • Kadıköy - Experience this vibrant neighborhood on the Asian side known for its markets and food scene

Duration: Full-day tour (approximately 9 hours)

Departure Time: 8:30 AM from your hotel

Return Time: Approximately 5:30 PM to your hotel

Price: 130 USD per person (minimum 2 persons)

Included: Hotel pickup and drop-off, air-conditioned vehicle, English-speaking guide, all entrance fees, Bosphorus ferry tickets, lunch at a traditional Turkish restaurant, and bottled water

Not Included: Personal expenses, additional food and beverages not specified, and gratuities

What to Bring: Comfortable walking shoes, camera, sun protection, and modest clothing for visiting religious sites (head covering for women when visiting mosques)
