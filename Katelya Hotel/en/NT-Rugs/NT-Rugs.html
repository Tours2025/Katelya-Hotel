Our Collection

Handmade Rugs & Kilims: They range from traditional, antique and tribal to contemporary rugs, in all sizes, colors and rich heritage patterns. We are holding a private collection of museum-quality and oversize original carpets dating back to the 16th century, naturally dyed with hand-spun virgin wool.

Ottoman Lamps: Our authentic mosaic lamps can be customized according to our guests' preferences — colors, patterns, and sizes tailored to your taste.

Double Knot Tradition: Carpets woven with Turkish double knots are more durable and long-lasting, ensuring your piece becomes a lasting heirloom.
