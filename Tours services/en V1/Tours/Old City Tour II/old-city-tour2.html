Our Old City Tour II continues the exploration of Istanbul's historic peninsula, focusing on the vibrant markets, underground wonders, and lesser-known architectural gems that complete the story of this magnificent city.

This tour is perfect for visitors who have already seen the main monuments or who wish to dive deeper into Istanbul's commercial and cultural heart. Wander through the labyrinthine Grand Bazaar, one of the world's oldest and largest covered markets, and immerse yourself in the aromatic wonderland of the Spice Bazaar.

You'll also discover the impressive engineering of the Basilica Cistern, the Byzantine underground water reservoir that once supplied the Great Palace of Constantinople. Our knowledgeable guides will bring these historic sites to life with fascinating stories and insights into Istanbul's rich past and vibrant present.
