Pamukkale Tour (Cotton Castle & Ancient Hierapolis)

Experience the breathtaking natural wonder of Pamukkale, known as the "Cotton Castle," and explore the ancient city of Hierapolis on this spectacular day tour from Istanbul. This UNESCO World Heritage site features stunning white terraces formed by calcium-rich mineral waters and the remarkably preserved ruins of a Greco-Roman spa city.

Tour Highlights:

  • Pamukkale Terraces - Walk barefoot on the brilliant white calcium terraces cascading down the hillside
  • Cleopatra's Ancient Pool - Opportunity to swim in the mineral-rich waters where Cleopatra herself once bathed (optional activity)
  • Ancient Hierapolis - Explore the well-preserved ruins of this ancient spa city including:
    • The magnificent Roman Theater
    • Apollo Temple
    • Necropolis (ancient cemetery)
    • Frontinus Street and City Gates
  • Hierapolis Archaeology Museum - View artifacts and exhibits from the region's rich history
  • Local Cuisine - Enjoy authentic Turkish lunch at a carefully selected local restaurant

Tour Options:

1. Day Trip by Flight (Same Day Return): Includes morning flight from Istanbul to Denizli, full-day tour, and evening return flight.

2. Overnight Tour (2 Days): Includes flights, tour, accommodation at a thermal spa hotel, and free time to enjoy the thermal waters in the evening.

Duration:

Day Trip: Approximately 16 hours (including flights)
Overnight Tour: 2 days, 1 night

Departure Time: Early morning flight from Istanbul (typically 6:00-7:00 AM)

Return Time:

Day Trip: Late evening flight to Istanbul (typically 8:00-9:00 PM)
Overnight Tour: Evening flight on the second day

Price:

Day Trip: 280 USD per person (minimum 2 persons)
Overnight Tour: 380 USD per person (minimum 2 persons)

Included: Hotel pickup and drop-off, round-trip domestic flights Istanbul-Denizli-Istanbul, air-conditioned vehicle, English-speaking guide, entrance fees to Pamukkale and Hierapolis, lunch, and bottled water. Overnight option also includes hotel accommodation and breakfast.

Not Included: Entrance to Cleopatra's Pool (approximately 15 USD), personal expenses, additional meals not specified, and gratuities

What to Bring: Comfortable walking shoes, swimwear and towel (if planning to swim), sun protection, camera, and passport/ID for domestic flights
