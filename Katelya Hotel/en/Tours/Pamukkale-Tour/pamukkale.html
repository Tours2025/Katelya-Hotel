Pamukkale Tour

Tour Itinerary:

  • Pick up from your hotel and drive to the airport
  • Our staff will meet you at Denizli Airport and take you to Pamukkale
  • Drive to Pamukkale, Kusadasi-Pamukkale duration is 3.5 hours
  • Our tour bus is a modern air-conditioned private bus
  • On arrival (approximately at 12:00) you will have open buffet lunch
  • After lunch the guided tour of Pamukkale will start

Visiting:

  • The ancient city of Hierapolis
  • Necropolis
  • Theatre
  • Roman Bath
  • Agora
  • Travertines
  • Pamukkale Natural Lake and Park

The tour will be finished at 4:00 pm, and then drive back to Kusadasi-Selcuk.
